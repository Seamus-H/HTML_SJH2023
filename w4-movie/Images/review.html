Review

Nichollette East Febuary 2, 2020


Seen this movie many times. I love it, it's so silly and ridiculous that it's great. Adam Sandler is a talented artist. His sound tracks to his many movies are exceptionally apt to the scene and the genre of audience.


In my opinion, Adam never fails to entertain me and makes me laugh in any of his movies. I particularly love his team that he takes with him. Stop, relax, grab and beer and fall under the spell of Sandler's movies that take you to a place that many other artists don't take their audience.


My husband and friend dressed and played like Happy for a fund raising day. The running hits are the best on screen and off. Congratulations Mr. Sandler for making a great movie that just keeps getting better with age.


G'day from Australia! :)
