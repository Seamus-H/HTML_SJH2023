Seamus Holloway Febuary 10, 2023


Seen this movie many times. I love it, it's so silly and ridiculous that it's great. Adam Sandler is a talented actor. His sound tracks to his many movies are exceptionally apt to the scene and the genre of audience.


In my opinion, Adam never fails to entertain me and makes me laugh in any of his movies. I particularly love his team that he takes with him. Stop, relax, and fall under the spell of Sandler's movies that take you to a place that many other artists don't take their audience.
